FAQ

What is Godel?

Welcome to the Godel Terminal, a professional web-based financial terminal software offering comprehensive financial and investment data.

Why should I use Godel?

Godel Terminal provides tools for financial and investment data at a very competitive price compared to other providers. You can access Godel Terminal anytime, anywhere, on any device. Unlike competitors, Godel Terminals Command Line Interface (CLI) system allows for instant gathering of realtime data such as charting, charts, news, trades, market quotes, and analysis in under 100 milliseconds.

What are the key features of Godel?

Godel Terminal features real-time charting powered by Nasdaq data, providing unlimited historical and intraday charts. It delivers realtime global news from hundreds of news wires and providers delivered in under 100 milliseconds. Users can access live market quotes, trades, and real-time prices directly from Nasdaq, as well as SEC filings with direct links to EDGAR from company inception, all updated in real-time. Additionally, the platform includes up-to-date company financial statements.
